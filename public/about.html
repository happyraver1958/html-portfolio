About Me

I'm an IT professional with over twenty (going on thirty) years of experience. I've had many positions over time in big and small companies. I started as a data entry operator in my early adult life and then I kept getting promoted as my supervisors saw I was interested in IT and not just a paycheck.

My greatest achievement is developing a Real Estate system from scratch based on an older, clunkier and outdated design. This project took about a year and a half and there was a lot of learning involved; I'm very happy my previous experience as a hobby developer helped a lot in understanding the concepts needed for this project. Unfortunately the company I worked for does not exist anymore but my experience remained with me and it wasn't just a challenge, it was an exciting process of learning new things and implementing them on the fly!

My last job had little to do with networks, it was more about project management, developing reports based on data sets and coordinating the development of scripts to automate processes for a team. It was extremely gratifying to work with such a great team! It is probably the best company I have ever worked for!
